Our Coffee

Apples on Ainsworth proudly serves Nat's Coffee, roasted in Byron Bay.

Nat's Batch 1 blend is a Colombian blend that is smooth and well balanced. A great milk coffee with just the right acidity. Batch 1 won a silver medal in the Golden Bean award in 2014.

The Apples on Ainsworth Story

Apples on Ainsworth started as a small suburban fruit shop. It is now one of Brisbane southside's most well known cafés.
